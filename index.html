The tour, in words

Where it began — Taylor's University

BSc (Hons) Computer Science, specialising in AI & Cybersecurity. Graduated December 2022 after a foundation year at Heriot-Watt University Dubai. Certified: Azure AZ-900 and CISI (UAE).

The building years — Al Ramz (2022–2025)

Joined the Robotics & Algorithms desk as a Software Engineer and grew into a Quantitative Developer role. Built a proprietary FIX engine for automated execution, Blazor risk dashboards, and margin frameworks.

Today — International Resources Holding, Quant Equities (since September 2025)

ML.NET models trading global equities, with Databricks research pipelines feeding the desk. Built an OTC options platform pricing Greeks, volatility surfaces, and IV term structure — deployed and generating signals in production.
